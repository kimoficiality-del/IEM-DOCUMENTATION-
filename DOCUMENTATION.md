# Infrastructure Engineering Masters Ltd. (IEM) - Documentation

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
**Website Purpose:** Official digital portal for IEM Ltd., showcasing engineering excellence and high-standard digital services in Bamenda.

**Business Goals:**
- Establish authority in civil and structural engineering.
- Generate leads for consultancy and project supervision.
- Provide a clear catalog for printing, data analysis, and project editing services.

**Brand Identity:**
- **Tagline:** "IEM is for durability and standard"
- **Colors:** Deep Engineering Blue, Clean White, Architectural Gray.

---

## 2. SITE STRUCTURE
- **Home:** Main landing page with trust signals.
- **Engineering Department:** Subsections for Infrastructure, Roads, Building, and Soil Testing.
- **Printing Services:** Document management and formatting.
- **Data Analysis:** SPSS/Excel statistical services.
- **Project Editing:** Academic and professional formatting.
- **Contact:** Multi-channel communication (WhatsApp, Phone, Form).

---

## 3. SEO & PERFORMANCE
- **Keywords:** Civil engineering Bamenda, Structural design Cameroon, Data analysis SPSS Cameroon.
- **Performance:** Full image optimization and code-splitting for fast mobile access in low-bandwidth areas.
